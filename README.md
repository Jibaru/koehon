# Koehon

Transform your PDF documents into interactive audio-enabled reading experiences with AI-powered translation.

## What is Koehon?

Koehon is a platform that converts PDF documents into an interactive reading experience. Upload your PDFs and Koehon will automatically extract text, translate it to your preferred language, and generate natural-sounding audio narration for each page.

## Key Features

### 📚 PDF Library
- Upload and organize your PDF documents
- Beautiful cover images automatically generated
- Quick access to your entire collection
- Bookmark your progress and resume where you left off

### 🌍 Multi-Language Translation
- Translate any PDF to your preferred language
- Support for multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, and more
- Preserve original formatting and structure
- AI-powered translations that maintain context and meaning

### 🎧 Audio Narration
- Natural-sounding text-to-speech for every page
- Listen while reading or on the go
- High-quality AI voices
- Track your listening hours

### 📖 Interactive Reading Experience
- Synchronized text and audio playback
- Navigate easily between pages
- Dark mode for comfortable reading
- Responsive design works on any device

### ⚙️ Personalization
- Use your own OpenAI API key for unlimited processing
- Create bookmarks to track your reading progress
- Pre-load pages in advance for faster access
- Organize and manage your document collection
